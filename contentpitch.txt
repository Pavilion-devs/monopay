**SLIDE 2 — The Problem (User Story)**
# Meet Amara

*Amara is a 34-year-old teacher in Lagos. Her brother sends her money from London every month. It takes 3 days, costs 8% in fees, and arrives in an amount she didn't expect because of exchange rates.*

*Her friend tells her about crypto. She downloads a wallet app. She sees this:*

***"Send to: 7xKp3...mN9qR2vL"***

*She closes the app and never opens it again.*

**Amara is not the exception. She is the majority.**

**SLIDE 4 — The Solution**
# Monopay: Show Simplicity. Hide Complexity.

Three things that change everything:

**① Nickname not wallet address**
Send to "@amara" not "7xKp3...mN9qR2vL"

**② Scan to Pay**
Merchants display a QR code. Customers scan and pay in seconds.

**③ Private transactions**
No one can see your amounts or activity on the blockchain explorer. It feels like cash.

*Powered by stablecoins — so it always feels like real money, never volatile crypto.*

## 💡 Design Tips When Building This

- **Slide 2** is your most important slide — make Amara's story visual, maybe show the scary wallet address in big red text
- **Slide 4** use big bold icons for each of the three features
- **Slide 5** is where screenshots do the heavy lifting — make them large and clean
- Keep every slide to **one dominant idea** — if you need to read it, it has too many words
- Color palette suggestion: clean white background, use the landing page theme and styling to design it. goodlick
